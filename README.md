<h1>Finance Forge Budget App</h1>

Welcome to the Finance Forge application. This app allows you to easily manage your household budget, making it simpler to continuously track expenses and adjust your financial plans accordingly. By regularly recording all your expenditures, you gain greater awareness of where your money goes, whether it's for a takeaway coffee or an electricity bill. This gives you full control over your finances and enables you to quickly respond to any changes. Regularly monitoring your expenses also helps catch unexpected costs, which is invaluable for maintaining a healthy budget balance. Start recording your expenses today and see how easy it is to achieve your financial goals!
